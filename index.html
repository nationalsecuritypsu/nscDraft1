The Pennylvania State University National Security Club fosters an engaging environment to prepare the next generation of government leaders. We provide Penn State students who aspire to pursue careers in national security with resources from Federal, State, and Local Law Enforcement agencies. This is facilitated through representatives and speakers from across the federal government in addition to other resources that the club may provide.
